About Me

The person
behind the work

I'm a product and innovation leader who has spent the last nine years at P&G India building things that reach millions of people. From establishing the country's first connected consumer research lab in Goa, to launching products that address real gaps in women's health — I care deeply about the quality of what gets made and why.

My work sits at the intersection of consumer insight and commercial delivery. I've led large-scale quantitative research, built competitive intelligence models, managed full product lifecycles, and coached teams across functions. The thread throughout: genuine curiosity about what people need, and the discipline to build it well.

Beyond P&G, I give time to social impact work — from civic tech NGOs to education programmes at IIT-Bombay. I believe good business and good impact aren't opposites; they're the same thing, done with enough ambition.

I'm now relocating to London and actively exploring senior Innovation, R&D, or Consumer Insights Director roles.
